What Syllabus is

Syllabus is a free weekly newsletter about artificial intelligence and robotics. Every week we scan the firehose of announcements, research, product launches, and breakthroughs, pick out what's genuinely important, and explain it in plain language — no jargon, no hype. It lands in your inbox every Sunday and takes about five minutes to read.

Why we built it

Most AI coverage is written either for engineers or for headlines. One is impenetrable, the other is empty. We wanted something in between: clear enough that a busy founder, operator, or curious reader can follow it, and useful enough that you walk away knowing what changed and what to do about it.

The goal is simple — help you stay ahead without having to live online all week.
