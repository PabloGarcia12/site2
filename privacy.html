Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Syllabus collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep this short and plain, the same way we write the newsletter.

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Who we are

Syllabus ("we", "us") is a free weekly newsletter covering artificial intelligence and robotics, published at syllabus.com and powered by RajeevKapur.ai. For any privacy question, contact us at admin@syllabus.com.

What we collect

When you subscribe, we collect:

  • Your email address — required to send you the newsletter.
  • Your name — only if you choose to provide it.
  • Engagement data — basic delivery and open/click activity reported by our email provider, used to improve the newsletter.

We do not ask for sensitive personal information, and we don't require an account to read Syllabus.

How we use it

  • To send you the weekly Syllabus newsletter and related updates.
  • To understand what content is useful so we can make the newsletter better.
  • To respond to you if you contact us.

We will never use your information for anything you haven't reasonably expected when you signed up.

Service providers

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to operate the newsletter. They process your information only to provide their service to us:

  • Flodesk — our email platform, which stores subscriber details and delivers the newsletter.
  • Cloudflare — hosting and content delivery for this website.
  • Google Workspace — for receiving and managing email we send to and from our own addresses.

Selling your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We don't share your email with advertisers or third parties for their own marketing.

Cookies & analytics

This site uses minimal cookies — only what's needed for it to function and load reliably (including Cloudflare's security and performance cookies). Newsletter emails may include standard open- and click-tracking pixels so we can see which topics resonate. You can block cookies in your browser settings and disable image loading in your email client if you prefer.

Your rights & choices

  • Unsubscribe anytime — every email includes a one-click unsubscribe link.
  • Access or correction — email us to see or update the information we hold about you.
  • Deletion — email us to have your information removed from our list.

Depending on where you live (for example, the EU/UK under GDPR, or California under the CCPA), you may have additional rights over your personal data. We honor these requests — just reach out to admin@syllabus.com.

Data retention

We keep your information for as long as you're subscribed. If you unsubscribe or ask to be deleted, we remove your details from our active list, retaining only what we're required to keep for legal or record-keeping reasons.

Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information and work with established providers who do the same. No method of transmission over the internet is ever completely secure, but we act to keep your data safe.

Children's privacy

Syllabus is intended for adults and is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has subscribed, contact us and we'll remove them.

International readers

Syllabus is operated from the United States. If you subscribe from elsewhere, your information may be processed in the U.S. and other countries where our service providers operate.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "last updated" date above. Significant changes will be communicated in the newsletter.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email admin@syllabus.com.
